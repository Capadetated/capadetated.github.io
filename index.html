Hello

I am a data scientist at Evonik residing in Boston, Massachusetts. Broadly, I am interested in mathematical optimization and large-scale computing. Specifically, I am happiest when I am designing, training, and deploying machine learning models in production settings. Application spaces of interest to me are graph machine learning, natural language processing, and "generative AI."

I completed my Ph.D. in mathematics at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute where I was advised by Yangyang Xu and mentored by Jie Chen. My research focus was on providing complexity analysis for first-order optimization methods in decentralized computing environments.

My resume is available upon request.
